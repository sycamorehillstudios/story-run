Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Sycamore Hill Studios ("we," "us," or "our") operates Story Run (the "App"), an AI‑powered GPS narrative running game available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Identifier

We generate and store a random, anonymous device identifier on your device. This ID links your profile, story progress, and subscription status. It is not tied to your name, email, or any account.

1.2 Location Data

While you are on an active run, we collect precise GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude, speed, and heading) to:

  • Generate narrative content that adapts to your real‑world surroundings (terrain, points of interest, time of day).
  • Display your route on an in‑app map.
  • Calculate run statistics (distance, pace).

Location is collected only during an active story run using an Android foreground service. We do not track your location in the background or when the App is closed.

1.3 Run and Game Data

We store run session data (distance, duration, pace, genre selected, story segments) and game progression data (player level, XP, inventory, quests, streaks) on our servers to provide the gameplay experience.

1.4 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to Story Run Premium, Google Play processes your payment. We receive a purchase token and product identifier to verify your subscription status. We do not collect or store credit card numbers or billing addresses.

1.5 Advertising Data

For free‑tier users, we display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Story Generation: GPS coordinates are sent to our server where an AI model generates narrative text adapted to your location context. Coordinates are not shared with any other third party.
  • Game Progression: Run and profile data power the leveling, inventory, quest, and streak systems.
  • Subscription Management: Purchase tokens verify and manage your Premium subscription.
  • Service Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage data (e.g., average run duration, popular genres) may be used to improve the App.

3. Third‑Party Services

We use the following third‑party services that may process your data:

  • Microsoft Azure (cloud hosting, AI story generation, text‑to‑speech, database) — Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Google AdMob (advertising for free‑tier users) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing (subscription payments) — Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

  • Run Sessions: Stored as long as you use the App so you can review your story library.
  • Player Profile: Retained as long as your device identifier is active.
  • GPS Coordinates: Stored as part of session data. Not used after the run concludes except to display your route history.

If you uninstall the App, your local device identifier is deleted. Server‑side data associated with that identifier will be automatically purged after 12 months of inactivity.

5. Data Security

We use HTTPS for all data transmission between the App and our servers. Server data is stored in Azure Cosmos DB with encryption at rest. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Story Run is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Location Permission: You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. The App requires location access to function during a run, but you can deny it when not running.
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings.
  • Data Deletion: To request deletion of all data associated with your device identifier, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
